About Us

Not so long ago, in 2012, we established a fast food restaurant in the Dunavska Street in Nis. Since then, we've been pacing steadily towards the goals we set, driven by ambition, constant search perfection and global standards we will not budge from.

Due to demand, two years later, we decided to move to a bigger space in a new location, at Bulevar Oslobodjenja, in close proximity to the railway and main coach station. This is where we opened a real Italian restaurant, with an authentic Italian wood-fired oven. Savoca Pizzeria & Ristorante quickly became synonymous with quality when it comes to the most popular cuisine in the world.
